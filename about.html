I'm a biomedical communicator and digital artist based in Toronto.

I utilize my background in science to communicate ideas in science and medicine through accurate, thoughtful, and compelling visuals. This can include 2D/3D animation, 3D modeling, illustration, design, and more. In particular, I love simplifying stories and concepts down to a digestible manner for the everyday person, while still retaining key scientific messages.

I currently work full-time as a medical animator at Klick Health, but feel free to send me a message for potential collaborations, or just to say hello!
