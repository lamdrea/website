MyRehab Concept & UI



MyRehab is a concept mobile application designed for rehabilitation patients.

Based on my personal experience working in a physiotherapy clinic, I noticed that patients often did not keep up with their prescribed exercises for various reasons: forgetting, laziness, losing their instruction sheet, not seeing improvement, and more. This impeded their recovery and made it difficult for their clinicians to assess the patient's progress as well as the effectiveness of the prescribed exercises.

To encourage patient and clinician engagement, MyRehab:

  • stores the patients' prescribed exercises
  • sends reminders
  • allows the user to log their exercise progress, in addition to daily pain
  • provides data visualizations for their progress
  • celebrates little milestones
